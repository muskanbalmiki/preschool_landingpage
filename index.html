Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents want to know — answered clearly.

🎒
What is the age criteria for admission?
Our programs accept children based on age as of June 1st of the academic year:
  • 🍼 Playgroup: 1.5 to 2.5 years
  • 🌱 Nursery: 2.5 to 3.5 years
  • 🚀 Junior KG: 3.5 to 4.5 years
  • 🎓 Senior KG: 4.5 to 5.5 years
If your child falls in between age groups, please call us — we assess each child individually.
📅
When do admissions open for 2026–27?
Admissions for 2026–27 are currently open! We begin accepting applications in November each year. Seats fill up quickly — contact us at +91 98765 43210.
💰
What are the fees and are there any hidden charges?
We believe in 100% transparent pricing. Annual fees range from ₹18,000 to ₹38,000. There are no hidden charges. EMI payments available and 10% sibling discount offered.
📄
What documents are needed for admission?
You need: child's birth certificate, 4 passport photos, parent's Aadhaar card, address proof, vaccination card, and previous school transfer certificate if applicable.
⏰
What are the school timings?
School runs Monday to Saturday:
  • 🍼 Playgroup: 8:30 AM – 11:00 AM
  • 🌱 Nursery: 8:30 AM – 11:30 AM
  • 🚀 Junior KG: 8:30 AM – 12:30 PM
  • 🎓 Senior KG: 8:30 AM – 1:30 PM
📚
What curriculum do you follow?
We follow a play-based, CBSE-aligned curriculum inspired by Montessori and NCF principles. Children learn through hands-on activities, stories, art, music and outdoor play.
🌍
Is the medium of instruction English or Hindi?
Our primary medium is English with a bilingual environment using Hindi alongside. We are sensitive to all mother-tongue backgrounds.
📊
How do you report a child's progress to parents?
  • 📱 Daily WhatsApp updates with photos
  • 📓 Monthly activity portfolio
  • 📋 Term-end report cards (twice a year)
  • 👩‍👧 PTMs every quarter
  • 📞 Open door policy — call anytime
🔒
How do you ensure my child's safety at school?
  • 📷 24/7 HD CCTV everywhere
  • 🔐 Biometric entry — verified adults only
  • 🚫 No-stranger policy
  • 🩺 First-aid trained teachers
  • ✅ Background-verified staff
🚌
Is transport available? Is it safe?
Yes! Door-to-door transport from Kharghar, Vashi, Panvel, Ulwe, Kamothe. GPS-tracked, female attendant on every bus. Fee: ₹1,200–1,800/month.
🍱
Do you provide lunch / tiffin?
We have a Healthy Tiffin Program (optional subscription). No junk food policy on campus. Parents can also send home-cooked tiffin.
📍
Where is LittleStars located?
Sector 10, Kharghar, Navi Mumbai – 410210. 5-minute walk from Kharghar Railway Station. Search "LittleStars Preschool Kharghar" on Google Maps.
🎯
Can I visit the school before enrolling?
Absolutely! Free school tours available Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM – 11:00 AM. Call +91 98765 43210 to schedule.
